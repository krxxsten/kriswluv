with luv

"more life, more memories, and more moments". these are the values i express within my short films. i record videos in the persepctive of life; my life. our memories are curated by the moments we live through, whether it is with family, friends, or our partners. i document these moments as a rememberance and homage to the little things that have made me who i am. i aspire to expand my media across different mediums; expanding outside the perspective of my life.
